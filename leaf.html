LEAF LAMP

BRIEF

Leaf is a lamp inspired by Frida Isola, an original lounge bar of Milan.

CONCEPT

Its use is a sort of game for the clients of the bar, who will bring it to the table to light it up during their permanence at the bar; creating, all together, a wonderful game of soft lights atmosphere, typical of Frida Isola at night.

DEVELOPMENT

Leaf is developed in partnership with Artemide and respects the main qualities of its products: elegance, linearity and environmental sustainability. Once rolled and fixed the lampshade up, using the special clips inside the packaging, it will be enough to place it on its basis pressing an easy mechanical joint.
